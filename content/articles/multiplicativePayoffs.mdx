Suppose you find a new game in your neighborhood casino.
It costs **$100** to play. Your bet is put into the pot. Your
payoff is determined by **10** consecutive coin tosses.

* After each heads the pot <span className="font-extrabold text-green-700">increases 50%</span>.
* After each tails the pot <span className="font-extrabold text-red-600">decreases 40%</span>.

After **10** tosses, whatever's in the pot is yours.
